## Story arc

Viktor Emter says that he and his wife decided around 2021/2022 to sell their apartment in Zirndorf. They initially attempted a private sale but received very few inquiries. They then spoke with Immonation and other agents.

He distinguishes Immonation by saying the initial conversation focused on listening to their expectations and timing rather than pressuring them into an immediate instruction. The couple requested time to think, held further meetings, and developed a rough sale plan covering timing, price expectations, and market conditions.

During the marketing period, rising interest rates made buyer financing more difficult. According to Emter, Immonation discussed the impact openly, explained that the sale could take longer, and agreed any price-strategy changes before implementing them. He describes the reasoning as understandable for non-specialists.

He praises the wider team for quick responses, proactive updates, and advance coordination of viewing dates. He says the sale eventually completed in a way that considered both seller and buyer needs. His overall assessment is that the company worked professionally, transparently, empathetically, and on a basis of trust; he would be highly likely to use Immonation again.
